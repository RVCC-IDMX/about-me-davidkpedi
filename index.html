Biography

I spend my time on personal projects and work on solving problems. i am both driven and self-motivated, and i am constantly experimenting with new technologies and techniques. i'm a natural team player, firmly believing that a team aligned around a common goal, is a successful team. Currently running a tech community on discord with discrete success.

As a web developer i am a fast, effecient and resourceful learner. with my abilities,skills and experience i am able to solve problems quickly. in my free time i enjoy spending time creating new designs for my streetwear brand (stubborngorilla) and making CSS art.
